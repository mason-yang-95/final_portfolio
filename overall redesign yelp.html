REDESIGN YELP

  • WHEN / WHEN / WHY

    This project is a project that finds the inconvenience of existing applications and redesigns them as a task at George Brown College in 2018. In the YELP app that I actually used a lot, I chose YELP because I felt uncomfortable. I went around the school with the group members and interviewed a total of 13 people, and newly designed wireframes and user flows.

    WHO

    Mason Myeoungryeul Yang, Ashely Jesso, Ahmed Mohamed, Avijit Saha

    WHAT

    Before starting this project, a preliminary survey was conducted with 13 people. When we surveyed people's reactions to YELP, some participants complained of discomfort due to the non-intuitive UI. It was difficult to add restaurants to the collection, and the UI was anachronistic. So, it has been transformed into an intuitive UI that everyone can use comfortably.

    PERSONA

    It was necessary to create various personas for realistic and re-design the existing application. There are a total of two personas, all of whom live in Toronto, but everything they want is different.

    ABOUT

    YELP is an application that many users use to find restaurants. However, the people we interviewed felt uncomfortable due to outdated designs, unnecessary assets, and not-easy-to-use UI. So we started this project.

    FINAL THOUGHTS

    It was my first group project at school. In fact, interviewing students who were studying at school was fresh, improved their English skills a little, and it was a good opportunity to understand the needs of users. As a result, satisfactory results were obtained by adjusting parts such as navigation bars and collection additions that are easy for users to use.
